# EyeTrax Changes — 2026-04-25

---

## 1. Dictionary upgrade — `english_words.csv`

**Files:** `src/eyetrax/app/keyboard_demo.py`

Replaced the old `wordFrequency.xlsx` (openpyxl-dependent) dictionary with `english_words.csv`.

- New file format: two columns — `word`, `freq` — sorted by frequency descending.
- Only the top **35,000** words are loaded (filtered to alphabetic-only, no duplicates).
- Words are ranked 1–35,000 by row order (rank 1 = most frequent). The trie sorts predictions by rank ascending, so the most common matching words surface first.
- `openpyxl` dependency removed from this path.

## 2. Vertical-only calibration mode

**Files:** `src/eyetrax/calibration/vertical_enhanced_calibration.py`, `src/eyetrax/calibration/__init__.py`, `src/eyetrax/calibration/multi_position.py`, `src/eyetrax/cli.py`

Added a new calibration mode `--calibration vertical-only`:

- 9 points along the **centre vertical line** only (x = screen_width // 2), evenly spaced 5%–95% of screen height.
- Trains a **1-D model** — targets are Y-coordinates only, no X prediction.
- Sets `gaze_estimator.vertical_only = True` and `gaze_estimator.vertical_center_x` so that at prediction time, X is fixed to screen centre and only Y is predicted.
- Instruction screen shown before calibration begins.
- Fully supported by `--multi-position` (runs with `multi_pose=True`).
- CLI: `--calibration vertical-only` added to choices.

## 4. Panel scroll-limit exit / backspace

**Files:** `src/eyetrax/app/keyboard_demo.py`

When inside a trie or bigram panel, navigation back to keyboard (or backspace) is triggered by **holding the scroll zone after the scroll is exhausted** — i.e., once you can't scroll any further, the timer starts:

| Panel | Zone | Scroll exhausted | Action |
|---|---|---|---|
| Trie | Top zone (look up) | At top of word list | 3 s → **backspace** + back to keyboard |
| Trie | Bottom zone (look down) | At bottom of word list | 2 s → **back to keyboard** (no word selected) |
| Bigram | Top zone (look up) | At top of word list | 2 s → **back to keyboard** |
| Bigram | Bottom zone (look down) | At bottom of word list | 3 s → **home screen (menu)** |

**Visual feedback:** A progress bar grows across the top or bottom edge of the screen while the timer counts. No text label — bar only.

**Bug fixed:** Previously the trie backspace timer started as soon as you looked at the top zone, even while actively scrolling. Fixed by adding the `at_top` guard — timer only starts once the scroll limit is reached.

**Opening gesture fix:** The off-screen opening gesture (`y < -40` or `y > height + 40`) no longer accidentally pre-arms the exit/backspace timer. Guards reset the timers whenever gaze is off-screen.

## 5. Panel word ordering

**Files:** `src/eyetrax/app/keyboard_demo.py`

- **Trie panel** (`TRIE_PANEL_ORDER = [4,3,2,1,0]`): best prediction (index 0) placed at the bottom display position. On open, scroll is initialised so the best word is at screen centre.
- **Bigram panel** (`NGRAM_PANEL_ORDER = [0,1,2,3,4]`): best prediction at the top display position. On open, scroll is initialised so the best word is at screen centre.
- Words above centre in trie = worse predictions; words below centre = closer to best.
- Words above centre in bigram = closer to best; words below = worse.

## 7. Long-blink action redesign

**Files:** `src/eyetrax/app/keyboard_demo.py`

| Blink duration | Action |
|---|---|
| **1 s** | Complete sentence → speak via TTS → clear text box |

## 8. Custom bigram from typed sentences

**Files:** `src/eyetrax/app/keyboard_demo.py`

Added a personal bigram system that learns from everything you type, independent of whether words came from suggestions:

### Recording
- On every **sentence completion** (1 s or 2 s blink), all consecutive word-pairs in the sentence are extracted and saved to `user_usage.json`.
- Example: typing "hello how are you" stores `hello→how +1`, `how→are +1`, `are→you +1`.
- Word-by-word `record_bigram()` calls removed from `_accept_word()` and `_replace_last_word()` — bigrams are now only recorded at sentence level.

### Prediction (`get_merged_bigram_predictions`)
When the bigram panel opens for word `prev`:
1. Look up personal bigram counts for `prev`, sort descending by count.
2. Fill up to 5 slots from personal data.
3. Fill any remaining slots from the static bigram model file (no duplicates).

Result: words you personally follow `prev` with appear first; the static model acts as a fallback.

## 12. Back gesture for non-keyboard screens

**Files:** `src/eyetrax/app/keyboard_demo.py`

All non-keyboard, non-menu screens now support a universal back gesture:

**Look above the screen (`y < -40`) for 3 seconds → return to home menu.**

Applies to: `fixed_phrases`, `lights_menu`, `mobile_phone`, `selection_mode`, `emergency_confirm`.

Does **not** apply to `keyboard` (which has its own panel navigation) or `menu` (already home).

A cyan progress bar grows across the top edge of the screen while the timer runs.
